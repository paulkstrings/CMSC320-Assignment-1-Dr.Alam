Q1: Why might we consider using git?

Answer: To keep track of changes to files and preserve backups of files at 
different states.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an example and how 
it might help.

Answer: Data science could be applied to the field of politics by letting 
political parties and candidates analyze poll data. This would let them 
make decisions about their policies.

Q3: What is the difference between merging and rebasing?

Answer: Merging preserves the entire history of the repository, while 
rebasing creates a linear history by moving your feature branch onto the 
tip of main.
